What to Expect in 2026

Modest Price Growth (2–4%)

Prices are expected to rise modestly statewide. Suburbs closest to downtown and ski canyons will likely see the strongest appreciation.

More Inventory Coming

New construction in Herriman, South Jordan, and Bluffdale is adding supply. Expect more options and slightly less competition in those areas.

Mortgage Rates Easing

Rates are expected to gradually improve through 2026, which could increase buyer demand. The best deals may be available in early 2026 before rate cuts bring more buyers in.

Best Buyer Opportunities

Herriman (new builds + inventory), South Jordan (slight softening), and Holladay (price correction) offer the most negotiating room for buyers right now.

A note about timing the market

Everyone wants to "time the market perfectly" — but the reality is that the best time to buy is when you're financially ready, you've found the right neighborhood, and the math works for your family. Waiting for a crash that may never come costs you in rising rents and missed appreciation. That said, if you're flexible on timing, late fall and winter in Utah consistently offer less competition and more negotiating power.
