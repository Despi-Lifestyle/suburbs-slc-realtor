How to use this chart

These numbers reflect 2025 market data and can shift month to month. Median prices represent the middle of the market — you'll find homes above and below these numbers in every suburb. The best way to narrow your search is to combine this data with your personal priorities: If schools are #1, focus on Canyons District suburbs (Draper, Sandy, Cottonwood Heights). If budget matters most, look at Herriman and Riverton. Want it all? Sandy is hard to beat for balance.
